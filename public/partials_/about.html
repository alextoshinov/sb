What is hike.io?

(scroll down)

It's all about hikes.

Detailed trail information, beautiful photos, full screen maps, GPX routes, and not much else.

It's editable.

Anyone can add new hikes or modify existing ones, without having to sign in.

It's free.

No paywalls or ads. Ever.

It's open.

Data is available under Creative Commons.
The code is open source.
